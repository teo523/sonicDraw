Welcome to SonicDraw!

Instructions:

  • USE A LAPTOP (no mobile phones).
  • Use Google Chrome or Firefox (Safari, Opera, Internet Explorer, etc. are not supported yet).
  • Use headphones/earphones and adjust the volume as you please.

Step 1. Training: Explore the interface and functions of the tool for at least 5 minutes as you like. If you want to start again, just reload the page.

Step 2. Creation: Create your own piece with SonicDraw. We recommend that you spend between 5 to 10 mins in this step. Once you have finished, please SHARE your piece with us by clicking on the envelope icon (right bottom corner).

After you have sent your piece, please go back to the survey form to complete the final stage of it.
